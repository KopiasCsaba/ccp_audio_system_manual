## Cable & microphone numbering on the stage

![stage.jpg](assets%2Fstage.jpg)

The logic is, that:
 * Close to **Mic1** is now **Inst1** and **Inst2** cables.
 * Close to **Mic3** is the **Inst3** cable.
 * And the stage corner has **Inst4** and **Inst5** cables.
